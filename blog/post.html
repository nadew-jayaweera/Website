Building neon-inspired UI without sacrificing performance

Jan 2026 7 min read Frontend

High-contrast gradients, glass panels, and motion can be performant when you lean on GPU-friendly properties, limit blur radius, and reuse tokens.

Start with design tokens for color, elevation, and radius. Use layered backgrounds instead of multiple shadows where possible, and reserve heavy blur for small elements. Animate transforms and opacity, and prefer will-change sparingly for interactive components.

For layouts, keep stacking contexts minimal, avoid over-nesting, and scope complex effects to isolated wrappers. Measure with the performance tab: watch FPS, layer count, and paint times as you iterate.
